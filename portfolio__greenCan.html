The Problem

Jon Winslow came to me during the summer of 2016, with a great product that lacked identity. He had great success with selling his product locally at trade shows as well as eBay. Mr. Winslow had an assortment of photos he had taken over the years from his cell phone but they were at low resolution. Without a logo or website, Jon lacked an identity for his product but we were able to lay out something not only he could be proud of but something that would continue to generate traffic towards his product.

Download Source

The Solution

Jon overnighted an ammunition can, equipped with his locking mechanism. I started by taking an assortment of photos with a DSLR camera, and proper studio lighting to show off the craftsmanship of the product, and quality of the product to the consumer. The ammunition can was painted a light military green, which I used as a base color for the branding color palette. When it came to the design of Mr. WInslow’s logo I wanted to emphasize the 20+ years he dedicated to the Fire department adding the fireman's hat. An obvious choice for the typography was the stencil-style lettering something often seen in gunman community. The goal for the website was to make something attractive but not complex, simplicity was key. I wanted the website to give professional appeal but put an emphasis on the key elements needed for engagement.
